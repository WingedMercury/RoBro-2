Competition

Each year has many competitions but only one ring event that year. The robot that you build will be based off that ring event,for that year. The ring event can be anything from: stacking and moving cones, throwing and pushing objects over and under walls, or practically anything within the capableites of a robot. Robot are built in teams of one to four.
